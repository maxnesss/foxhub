16 Personalities Test

The 16 Personalities Test, also known as the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), is a widely-used psychometric tool designed to assess an individual's personality traits and preferences. It is based on the theory proposed by Carl Jung, a Swiss psychiatrist, and was later developed by Isabel Briggs Myers and Katharine Cook Briggs.

The test categorizes individuals into 16 different personality types, each represented by a four-letter code.

We recommend to complete the test for information crucial for your profile.
